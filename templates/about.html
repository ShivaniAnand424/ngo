Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Jyeshtha Nagarik Pratishthan (JNP) is a registered charitable organization dedicated to the welfare and empowerment of senior citizens, differently-abled individuals, and orphaned communities in Nagpur.

Under the visionary leadership of our President, Hon. Shri Nitin Gadkari, we work to ensure every elder lives a life of dignity, health, and joy.

Our Journey

Milestones Over the Years

2017

Foundation Established

JNP was founded with a mission to serve senior citizens of Nagpur under the leadership of Hon. Shri Nitin Gadkari.

2019

100+ Mandals Connected

Expanded our network to over 100 local senior citizen mandals across Nagpur district.

2021

Legal & Medical Centers Launched

Free legal counseling and medical camps started for all registered senior members.

2024 – Present

20,000+ Members & Growing

Today we proudly serve over 20,000 senior citizens through 203+ mandals across the region.
